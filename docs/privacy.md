## PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated June 24, 2026

This Privacy Notice for Moravian University Computer Science Program ("we," "us," or "our"), describes how and why we collect your personal information when you use the Hue SMS Control service.

**What personal information do we process?** When you send an SMS message to us, we see your phone number on the content of your message. 

**Do we collect any information from third parties?**  We do not collect any information from third parties.

**How do we process your information?** We save the content of your message and the **last four digits** of your phone number for logging purposes.

**In what situations and with which parties do we share personal information?**  We **NEVER** share your information with third parties.

**How do we keep your information safe?** We only save the **last four digits** of your phone number.  This information is not sufficient to identify an individual.
